About Me

I’m a Data Analyst with over 2.5 years of experience helping businesses grow by using data to solve problems and make better decisions. Right now, I’m working at Quinte Financial Technologies, where I focus on analyzing loan data in the banking sector. My role involves creating detailed reports and building interactive dashboards that provide important insights for decision-making. I work closely with different teams to understand what data they need and deliver it in a way that’s easy to use and understand.

Before this, I worked at NielsenIQ, where I helped large companies like Walmart, P&G, Nestle, and PepsiCo improve their sales and market share. I did this by analyzing their sales data, identifying trends, and providing insights to help them make better business decisions. I also automated several reports and created dashboards that helped my clients track performance and quickly identify areas for improvement.

I enjoy working with a variety of tools, including Power BI, Python, SQL, and Excel. These tools help me clean, organize, and analyze data so that I can turn it into valuable insights. Whether it’s spotting trends, building data models, or creating visual reports, I’m passionate about making data accessible and understandable for everyone. My goal is to help businesses use data to solve problems, improve processes, and make more informed decisions.

I’m always excited to learn new skills and stay updated with the latest trends in data analysis. I believe that data has the power to change the way businesses operate, and I love being part of that change. I’m also a strong believer in collaboration and enjoy working with others to figure out the best ways to use data to achieve business goals.

I’m actively looking for new opportunities to apply my skills in data and business analytics to create impactful, data-driven solutions. Let’s connect and see how I can contribute to your organization’s success!
